About Me

I started off on a Pre-PT track straight out of high school and did that for two years before I realized that it wasn't for me. I then attended Ball State's Computer Science program for a year until the Covid-19 pandemic broke out. I was given a wonderful opportunity to attend Eleven Fifty Academy so that I could gain the knowledge and experience that I needed to switch fields. I now have various programming and problem solving skills that could be of great value to any company looking to hire a junior developer.
